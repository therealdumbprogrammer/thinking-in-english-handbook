# Pattern Family 18 — Interrupting Politely

## Hindi Pattern

- एक मिनट...
- बीच में बोल रहा हूँ...
- माफ़ कीजिए, लेकिन...
- बीच में टोक रहा हूँ...

## Natural English Expressions

- Sorry to interrupt.
- Excuse me.
- If I may...
- Can I jump in for a second?
- May I add something?

## Nuance

Use these expressions to enter a conversation politely without sounding rude.

## Examples

- Sorry to interrupt, but I have a quick question.
- Excuse me, may I add something?
- Can I jump in for a second?

## Common Mistakes

❌ I am cutting your talk.

✅ Sorry to interrupt.

## Memory Tip

> Apologize briefly before interrupting.
