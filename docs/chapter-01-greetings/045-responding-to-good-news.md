# Pattern Family 45 — Responding to Good News

## Hindi Pattern

- बधाई हो!
- वाह!
- बहुत बढ़िया!

## Natural English Expressions

- Congratulations!
- That's great!
- Fantastic!
- Good for you!
- That's wonderful!

## Nuance

Celebrate naturally with short enthusiastic responses.

## Examples

- Congratulations!
- That's great!
- Fantastic!

## Common Mistakes

See the preferred expressions above instead of translating literally.

## Memory Tip

> Learn the expression as a complete phrase.
