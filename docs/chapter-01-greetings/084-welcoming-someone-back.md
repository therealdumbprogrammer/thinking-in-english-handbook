# Pattern Family 84 — Welcoming Someone Back

## Hindi Pattern

- वापस आने पर स्वागत है।
- अरे, वापस आ गए!
- फिर से स्वागत है।

## Natural English Expressions

- Welcome back!
- Good to have you back.
- Nice to see you again.
- Glad you're back.
- Welcome back, everyone.

## Nuance

Used after someone returns.

## Examples

- Welcome back!
- Good to have you back.
- Nice to see you again.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Learn these as complete spoken expressions.
