# Pattern Family 13 — Excusing Yourself Before Speaking

## Hindi Pattern

- एक बात पूछनी थी।
- एक बात कहनी थी।
- अगर बुरा न मानो...
- परेशान तो नहीं कर रहा?

## Natural English Expressions

- Can I ask you something?
- I wanted to ask you something.
- Do you have a quick second?
- I hope I'm not bothering you.
- Can I run something by you?

## Nuance

Used to soften the start of a conversation or request.

## Examples

- Can I ask you something?
- I hope I'm not bothering you.
- Do you have a quick second?

## Common Mistakes

❌ One thing I wanted to tell.

✅ I wanted to tell you something.

## Memory Tip

> Soften the opening before making your request.
