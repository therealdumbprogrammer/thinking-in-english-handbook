# Pattern Family 80 — Warm Goodbyes

## Hindi Pattern

- अपना ख़याल रखना।
- फिर मिलेंगे।
- बाय, ध्यान रखना।

## Natural English Expressions

- Take care.
- Have a wonderful day.
- See you soon.
- Bye for now.
- It was great talking to you.

## Nuance

Friendly endings for personal and professional conversations.

## Examples

- Take care.
- Have a wonderful day.
- See you soon.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Treat these as complete spoken expressions.
