# Pattern Family 44 — Checking Readiness

## Hindi Pattern

- तैयार?
- चलें?
- हो गया?

## Natural English Expressions

- Ready?
- Are you ready?
- Shall we?
- Good to go?
- Can we get started?

## Nuance

Used before beginning an activity.

## Examples

- Ready?
- Are you ready?
- Shall we?

## Common Mistakes

See the preferred expressions above instead of translating literally.

## Memory Tip

> Learn the expression as a complete phrase.
