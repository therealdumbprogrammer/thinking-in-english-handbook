# Pattern Family 1 — Basic Greetings

## Hindi Pattern

- नमस्ते।
- हेलो।
- हाय।
- अरे, नमस्ते!
- अरे, हाय!

---

## Natural English Expressions

| Expression | Register | Notes |
|------------|----------|-------|
| Hi! | Casual | The most common everyday greeting. |
| Hello! | Neutral | Appropriate almost everywhere. |
| Hey! | Casual | Friendly and informal. |
| Good morning! | Neutral | Morning greeting. |
| Good afternoon! | Neutral | Afternoon greeting. |
| Good evening! | Neutral | Evening greeting. |

---

## Nuance

- "Hi!" is the safest everyday greeting.
- "Hello!" is slightly more neutral.
- "Hey!" is informal.
- Time-based greetings are common in workplaces and customer-facing situations.

---

## Examples

### 🏠 Home

**Hindi:** अरे, हाय! खाना तैयार है।

**Natural English:** Hi! Dinner's ready.

### 💼 Office

**Hindi:** सुप्रभात सभी को।

**Natural English:** Good morning, everyone.

### 👥 Friends

**Hindi:** अरे! बहुत दिनों बाद!

**Natural English:** Hey! Long time no see!

### 📱 Phone

**Hindi:** हेलो, क्या मैं राहुल से बात कर सकता हूँ?

**Natural English:** Hello! May I speak to Rahul?

### 🛍️ Shop

**Hindi:** नमस्ते! मैं आपकी कैसे मदद कर सकता हूँ?

**Natural English:** Hello! How can I help you?

---

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake

Hello, myself Rahul.

### ✅ Natural English

Hi! I'm Rahul.

### Why?

Native speakers introduce themselves with "I'm...", not "Myself...".

---

## Similar English Expressions

| Expression | Difference |
|------------|------------|
| Hi there! | Slightly warmer. |
| Hey there! | More informal. |
| Morning! | Casual version of "Good morning." |

---

## Memory Tip

> When in doubt, say **Hi!**

## Mini Practice

---

## Quick Recap

**Most common:** Hi!

**Most formal:** Good morning!

**Avoid:** Hello, myself Rahul.

**Remember:** English greetings usually continue with small talk.
