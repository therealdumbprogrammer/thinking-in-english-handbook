# Pattern Family 73 — Handling a Bad Connection

## Hindi Pattern

- नेटवर्क नहीं है।
- आवाज़ टूट रही है।
- सिग्नल कमजोर है।

## Natural English Expressions

- The connection is bad.
- You're breaking up.
- I can barely hear you.
- The signal is weak.
- The line keeps cutting out.

## Nuance

Useful during phone and video calls.

## Examples

- The connection is bad.
- You're breaking up.
- I can barely hear you.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Memorize the whole expression.
