# Pattern Family 9 — Nice to Meet You

## Hindi Pattern

- आपसे मिलकर खुशी हुई।
- आपसे मिलकर अच्छा लगा।
- मिलकर अच्छा लगा।
- अच्छा लगा मिलकर।

---

## Natural English Expressions

| Expression | Register | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Nice to meet you. | Neutral | First meeting. |
| Nice meeting you. | Neutral | Usually when leaving. |
| It was great meeting you. | Casual | Warm farewell. |
| It was a pleasure meeting you. | Formal | Professional. |
| Great to see you. | Casual | For someone you've met before. |

---

## Nuance

Use **Nice to meet you** during introductions. Use **Nice meeting you** when ending the conversation. **Great to see you** is for people you already know.

---

## Examples

### 💼 Interview

**Hindi:** आपसे मिलकर खुशी हुई।

**Natural English:** It was a pleasure meeting you.

### 🤝 Networking Event

**Hindi:** आपसे मिलकर अच्छा लगा।

**Natural English:** Nice to meet you.

### 👥 Friends

**Hindi:** फिर से मिलकर अच्छा लगा।

**Natural English:** Great to see you again.

### 🏠 New Neighbour

**Hindi:** मैं आपका नया पड़ोसी हूँ। मिलकर अच्छा लगा।

**Natural English:** I'm your new neighbour. Nice to meet you.

### 📞 Business Meeting

**Hindi:** आज आपसे मिलकर अच्छा लगा।

**Natural English:** It was great meeting you today.

---

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake

I am happy after meeting you.

### ✅ Natural English

Nice meeting you.

### Why?

English uses established expressions rather than describing your feelings literally.

---

## Similar English Expressions

| Expression | Difference |
|---|---|
| Glad we met. | Informal. |
| Lovely meeting you. | Warmer. |
| Great seeing you again. | Reunion. |

---

## Memory Tip

> **Meet** = first meeting. **See** = someone you already know.

## Quick Recap

**First meeting:** Nice to meet you.

**Leaving:** Nice meeting you.

**Avoid:** I am happy after meeting you.
