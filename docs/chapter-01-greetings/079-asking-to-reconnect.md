# Pattern Family 79 — Reconnecting After a Call Drops

## Hindi Pattern

- कॉल कट गई।
- मैं दोबारा कॉल करता हूँ।
- फिर से जोड़ता हूँ।

## Natural English Expressions

- The call got disconnected.
- I'll call you back.
- Let me reconnect.
- I'll ring you again.
- Let's try again.

## Nuance

Useful after connection issues.

## Examples

- The call got disconnected.
- I'll call you back.
- Let me reconnect.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Treat these as complete spoken expressions.
