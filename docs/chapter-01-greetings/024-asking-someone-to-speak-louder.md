# Pattern Family 24 — Asking Someone to Speak Louder

## Hindi Pattern
- ज़रा ज़ोर से बोलो।
- आवाज़ नहीं आ रही।
- थोड़ा ऊँचा बोलिए।

## Natural English Expressions

- Could you speak up?
- I can't hear you.
- Could you speak a little louder?
- Your voice is very soft.

## Nuance

"Speak up" is the most natural everyday expression.

## Examples

- Could you speak up?
- I can't hear you.

## Common Mistakes

❌ Speak loudly.

✅ Could you speak up?

## Memory Tip

> "Speak up" is the idiomatic choice.
