# Pattern Family 2 — Asking How Someone Is

## Hindi Pattern

- कैसे हो?
- कैसी हो?
- कैसे हैं?
- क्या हाल है?
- क्या हाल-चाल है?

---

## Natural English Expressions

| Expression | Register | Notes |
|------------|----------|-------|
| How are you? | Neutral | Universal greeting. |
| How's it going? | Casual | Very common. |
| How've you been? | Casual | After a long gap. |
| How are things? | Neutral | Broader than personal well-being. |
| What's up? | Casual | Informal greeting. |

---

## Nuance

These expressions are usually greetings, not genuine requests for detailed updates. A short answer is expected.

---

## Examples

### 👥 Friends

**Hindi:** अरे! कैसे हो?

**Natural English:** Hey! How's it going?

### 💼 Office

**Hindi:** गुड मॉर्निंग! कैसे हैं?

**Natural English:** Good morning! How are you?

### 📱 Phone

**Hindi:** हेलो! बहुत दिनों बाद। कैसे हो?

**Natural English:** Hi! How've you been?

### 🏠 Home

**Hindi:** सब कैसा चल रहा है?

**Natural English:** How are things?

### ☕ Café

**Hindi:** क्या हाल है?

**Natural English:** What's up?

---

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake

What is your condition?

### ✅ Natural English

How are you?

### Why?

"Condition" refers to health or a situation, not a normal greeting.

---

## Similar English Expressions

| Expression | Difference |
|------------|------------|
| Everything okay? | Checks if something is wrong. |
| You okay? | More personal. |
| What's new? | Asks for recent updates. |

---

## Memory Tip

> Treat "How are you?" as a greeting, not an interview.

## Quick Recap

**Most common:** How are you?

**Most casual:** What's up?

**Avoid:** What is your condition?

**Remember:** Keep your reply short.
