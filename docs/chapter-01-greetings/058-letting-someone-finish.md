# Pattern Family 58 — Letting Someone Finish

## Hindi Pattern

- पहले तुम बोलो।
- पूरी बात कहो।
- मैं सुन रहा हूँ।

## Natural English Expressions

- Go ahead.
- Please finish.
- I'm listening.
- Take your time.
- Carry on.

## Nuance

Encourages the speaker to continue without interruption.

## Examples

- Go ahead.
- Please finish.
- I'm listening.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations; use the established English expressions.

## Memory Tip

> Learn the expression as a complete chunk.
