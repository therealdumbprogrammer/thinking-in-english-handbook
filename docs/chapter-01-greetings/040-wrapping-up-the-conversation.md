# Pattern Family 40 — Wrapping Up the Conversation

## Hindi Pattern

- ठीक है फिर।
- चलो फिर मिलते हैं।
- अच्छा, चलता हूँ।

## Natural English Expressions

- Alright then.
- Well, I'd better get going.
- It was nice talking to you.
- See you later.
- Take care.

## Nuance

End conversations politely and positively.

## Examples

- Alright then.
- Well, I'd better get going.
- It was nice talking to you.

## Common Mistakes

❌ Okay bye only.

✅ Alright then, take care.

## Memory Tip

> Finish warmly.
