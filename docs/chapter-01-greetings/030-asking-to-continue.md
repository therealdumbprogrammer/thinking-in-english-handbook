# Pattern Family 30 — Inviting Someone to Continue

## Hindi Pattern

- आगे बताओ।
- फिर?
- उसके बाद?

## Natural English Expressions

- Go on.
- Please continue.
- And then?
- What happened next?
- I'm listening.

## Nuance

Encourages the speaker to continue.

## Examples

- Go on.
- Please continue.
- And then?

## Common Mistakes

❌ Tell further.

✅ Go on.

## Memory Tip

> 'Go on' is the natural prompt.
