# Pattern Family 3 — Responding to Greetings

## Hindi Pattern

- मैं ठीक हूँ।
- बढ़िया हूँ।
- सब बढ़िया।
- ठीक-ठाक हूँ।
- मज़े में हूँ।

---

## Natural English Expressions

| Expression | Register | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| I'm good. | Casual | Most common reply. |
| I'm doing well. | Neutral | Slightly more formal. |
| Pretty good. | Casual | Positive. |
| Not bad. | Casual | Means fairly good. |
| I'm doing great. | Casual | Very positive. |

---

## Nuance

English speakers usually keep responses short unless invited to elaborate.

---

## Examples

### 👥 Friends

**Hindi:** मैं बढ़िया हूँ। तुम बताओ?

**Natural English:** I'm good. How about you?

### 💼 Office

**Hindi:** मैं ठीक हूँ, धन्यवाद।

**Natural English:** I'm doing well, thanks.

### 📱 Phone

**Hindi:** सब बढ़िया है।

**Natural English:** Pretty good.

### 🏠 Home

**Hindi:** मैं ठीक-ठाक हूँ।

**Natural English:** Not bad.

### ☕ Café

**Hindi:** मैं मज़े में हूँ।

**Natural English:** I'm doing great.

---

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake

I am fine only.

### ✅ Natural English

I'm good.

### Why?

"Only" is unnecessary and sounds unnatural here.

---

## Similar English Expressions

| Expression | Difference |
|---|---|
| Doing alright. | Neutral. |
| So far, so good. | Things are going well. |
| Couldn't be better. | Extremely positive. |

---

## Memory Tip

> Default to **I'm good.**

## Mini Practice

### Hindi → English

मैं ठीक हूँ, धन्यवाद।

### English → Hindi

Pretty good, thanks.

---

## Quick Recap

**Most common:** I'm good.

**Avoid:** I am fine only.

**Remember:** Keep it short.
