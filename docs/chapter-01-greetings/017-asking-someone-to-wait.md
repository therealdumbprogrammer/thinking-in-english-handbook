# Pattern Family 17 — Asking Someone to Wait

## Hindi Pattern

- एक मिनट।
- ज़रा रुको।
- थोड़ी देर रुको।
- बस दो मिनट।

## Natural English Expressions

- Just a second.
- Hang on.
- Hold on.
- Give me a minute.
- One moment, please.

## Nuance

Choose expressions based on formality. "Hang on" is casual; "One moment, please" is more formal.

## Examples

- Hang on, I'm coming.
- Just a second.
- Give me a minute.

## Common Mistakes

❌ Wait.

✅ Just a second.

## Memory Tip

> Native speakers often soften "wait" with "just" or "one moment."
