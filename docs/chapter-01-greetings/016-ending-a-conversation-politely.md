# Pattern Family 16 — Ending a Conversation Politely

## Hindi Pattern

- अच्छा, चलता हूँ।
- ठीक है, फिर बात करते हैं।
- अब निकलता हूँ।
- चलता हूँ फिर।

## Natural English Expressions

- I'd better get going.
- I'll let you go.
- Talk to you later.
- See you later.
- I've got to run.

## Nuance

Native speakers often soften departures instead of saying "I'm leaving."

## Examples

- I'd better get going.
- I'll let you get back to work.
- Talk to you later.

## Common Mistakes

❌ I am going.

✅ I'd better get going.

## Memory Tip

> End naturally instead of announcing you're leaving.
