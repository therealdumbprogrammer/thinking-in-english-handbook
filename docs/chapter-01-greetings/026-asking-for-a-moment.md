# Pattern Family 26 — Asking for a Moment

## Hindi Pattern

- एक सेकंड।
- बस एक मिनट।
- थोड़ा समय दो।

## Natural English Expressions

- Just a moment.
- Give me a second.
- One moment, please.
- Bear with me.
- Give me a minute.

## Nuance

Use these when you need a brief pause before continuing.

## Examples

- Just a moment.
- Give me a second.
- One moment, please.

## Common Mistakes

❌ Wait.

✅ Just a moment.

## Memory Tip

> Use 'just' to soften the request.
