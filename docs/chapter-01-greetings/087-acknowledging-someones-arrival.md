# Pattern Family 87 — Acknowledging Someone's Arrival

## Hindi Pattern

- अरे, आ गए!
- आइए, आइए।
- आपका इंतज़ार था।

## Natural English Expressions

- There you are!
- Glad you could make it.
- Welcome!
- Good to see you.
- We were just waiting for you.

## Nuance

Friendly ways to greet someone who has just arrived.

## Examples

- There you are!
- Glad you could make it.
- Welcome!

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Learn the expression as a complete conversational chunk.
