# Pattern Family 14 — Getting Someone's Attention

## Hindi Pattern

- सुनो...
- ज़रा सुनना...
- अरे सुनो...
- एक मिनट...

## Natural English Expressions

- Excuse me.
- Hey.
- Listen.
- Can I get your attention for a second?
- One second.

## Nuance

Choose expressions based on familiarity and formality.

## Examples

- Excuse me, could you help me?
- Hey, listen.
- One second, please.

## Common Mistakes

❌ Listen me.

✅ Listen.

✅ Listen to me.

## Memory Tip

> It's "listen to someone", not "listen someone".
