# Pattern Family 75 — Promising to Follow Up

## Hindi Pattern

- मैं बताता हूँ।
- मैं अपडेट दूँगा।
- मैं वापस बताऊँगा।

## Natural English Expressions

- I'll let you know.
- I'll get back to you.
- I'll keep you posted.
- I'll update you.
- I'll follow up.

## Nuance

Common business and everyday follow-up phrases.

## Examples

- I'll let you know.
- I'll get back to you.
