# Pattern Family 32 — Showing Interest

## Hindi Pattern

- अच्छा?
- सच में?
- फिर?

## Natural English Expressions

- Really?
- Is that so?
- No way!
- Tell me more.
- What happened next?

## Nuance

Shows curiosity and keeps the conversation going.

## Examples

- Really?
- Is that so?
- No way!

## Common Mistakes

❌ Tell further.

✅ Tell me more.

## Memory Tip

> Ask naturally to encourage the speaker.
