# Pattern Family 70 — Ending a Phone Call

## Hindi Pattern

- ठीक है, फिर बात करते हैं।
- चलो, रखता हूँ।
- बाय।

## Natural English Expressions

- Talk to you later.
- I'll let you go.
- Take care.
- Bye for now.
- Have a good day.

## Nuance

Polite ways to end calls.
