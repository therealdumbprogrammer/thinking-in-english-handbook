# Pattern Family 20 — Confirming You Understand

## Hindi Pattern

- समझ गया।
- ठीक है।
- अच्छा।
- समझ में आ गया।

## Natural English Expressions

- Got it.
- I understand.
- That makes sense.
- Okay, I see.
- Understood.

## Nuance

Choose the expression based on formality. "Got it" is conversational; "Understood" is more formal.

## Examples

- Got it. I'll do that.
- Okay, I see.
- That makes sense.

## Common Mistakes

❌ I understood.

✅ I understand. / Got it.

## Memory Tip

> Use "Got it." for everyday conversations.
