# Pattern Family 8 — Where Have You Been?

## Hindi Pattern

- कहाँ गायब थे?
- कहाँ थे इतने दिन?
- दिख ही नहीं रहे थे।
- नज़र ही नहीं आ रहे थे।

---

## Natural English Expressions

| Expression | Register | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Where have you been? | Casual | Most common. |
| Haven't seen you in a while. | Neutral | Warm and friendly. |
| You've disappeared! | Casual | Playful exaggeration. |
| I was wondering where you were. | Neutral | Gentle. |
| Good to see you again. | Neutral | Positive reunion. |

---

## Nuance

These expressions communicate that someone has been absent and that you've noticed it.

---

## Examples

### 👥 Friends

**Hindi:** कहाँ गायब थे?

**Natural English:** Where have you been?

### 💼 Office

**Hindi:** काफी दिनों से नहीं दिखे।

**Natural English:** Haven't seen you in a while.

### 🏋️ Gym

**Hindi:** आजकल दिखते ही नहीं।

**Natural English:** You've disappeared!

### 🏠 Neighbour

**Hindi:** सोच रहा था कहाँ थे।

**Natural English:** I was wondering where you were.

### 📱 Chat

**Hindi:** बहुत दिनों बाद ऑनलाइन दिखे।

**Natural English:** Good to see you again.

---

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake

You were not visible.

### ✅ Natural English

Haven't seen you in a while.

### Why?

English focuses on not seeing the person, not them being "visible."

---

## Similar English Expressions

| Expression | Difference |
|---|---|
| Long time no see. | Greeting after meeting. |
| Missed seeing you. | More emotional. |

---

## Memory Tip

> Say **Haven't seen you...**, not **You were not visible**.

## Quick Recap

**Most common:** Where have you been?

**Avoid:** You were not visible.
