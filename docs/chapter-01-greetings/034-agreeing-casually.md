# Pattern Family 34 — Agreeing Casually

## Hindi Pattern

- हाँ सही है।
- बिल्कुल।
- ठीक है।

## Natural English Expressions

- Exactly.
- Absolutely.
- I agree.
- Sounds good.
- You're right.

## Nuance

Common responses in everyday conversations.

## Examples

- Exactly.
- Absolutely.
- I agree.

## Common Mistakes

❌ I am agree.

✅ I agree.

## Memory Tip

> Never use 'I am agree'.
