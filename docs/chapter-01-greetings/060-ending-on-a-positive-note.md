# Pattern Family 60 — Ending on a Positive Note

## Hindi Pattern

- ख़याल रखना।
- फिर मिलेंगे।
- दिन अच्छा रहे।

## Natural English Expressions

- Take care.
- Have a great day.
- See you soon.
- Have a good one.
- Enjoy the rest of your day.

## Nuance

Friendly closing expressions.

## Examples

- Take care.
- Have a great day.
- See you soon.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations; use the established English expressions.

## Memory Tip

> Learn the expression as a complete chunk.
