# Pattern Family 5 — Responding to Thanks

## Hindi Pattern

- कोई बात नहीं।
- कोई दिक्कत नहीं।
- अरे, इसकी क्या बात है।
- कभी भी।

---

## Natural English Expressions

| Expression | Register | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| You're welcome. | Neutral | Standard response. |
| No problem. | Casual | Very common. |
| No worries. | Casual | Relaxed and friendly. |
| Anytime. | Casual | Happy to help again. |
| My pleasure. | Formal | Polite and professional. |

---

## Nuance

English has fixed responses after someone says "Thank you." Choose the one that matches the situation.

---

## Examples

### 💼 Office

**Hindi:** धन्यवाद!

**Natural English:** You're welcome.

### 👥 Friends

**Hindi:** थैंक्स यार!

**Natural English:** No problem.

### 🏠 Home

**Hindi:** शुक्रिया!

**Natural English:** No worries.

### 🍽️ Restaurant

**Hindi:** धन्यवाद।

**Natural English:** My pleasure.

### 🚗 Friends

**Hindi:** लिफ्ट देने के लिए धन्यवाद।

**Natural English:** Anytime.

---

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake

There is no problem.

### ✅ Natural English

No problem.

### Why?

Native speakers use the fixed expression "No problem."

---

## Similar English Expressions

| Expression | Difference |
|---|---|
| Happy to help. | Friendly. |
| Don't mention it. | Polite. |
| It was nothing. | Downplays the favor. |

---

## Memory Tip

> After "Thank you", your safest reply is **You're welcome.**

## Quick Recap

**Most common:** You're welcome.

**Most casual:** No problem.

**Avoid:** There is no problem.
