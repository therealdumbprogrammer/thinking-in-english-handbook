# Pattern Family 41 — Asking for Clarification

## Hindi Pattern

- मतलब?
- क्या मतलब?
- ज़रा समझाओ।

## Natural English Expressions

- What do you mean?
- Could you clarify?
- Could you explain that?
- I'm not sure I follow.
- What exactly do you mean?

## Nuance

Use polite clarification instead of assuming.

## Examples

- What do you mean?
- Could you clarify?
- Could you explain that?

## Common Mistakes

See the preferred expressions above instead of translating literally.

## Memory Tip

> Learn the expression as a complete phrase.
