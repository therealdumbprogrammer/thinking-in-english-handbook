# Pattern Family 78 — Checking Audio Status

## Hindi Pattern

- आप म्यूट पर हैं।
- आवाज़ नहीं आ रही।
- माइक ऑन कीजिए।

## Natural English Expressions

- I think you're on mute.
- I can't hear you.
- Could you unmute yourself?
- Your microphone seems to be muted.
- Your audio isn't coming through.

## Nuance

Common in online meetings.
