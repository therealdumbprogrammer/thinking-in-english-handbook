# Pattern Family 67 — Requesting a Callback

## Hindi Pattern

- बाद में कॉल करना।
- फुर्सत मिले तो फोन करना।
- वापस कॉल कर देना।

## Natural English Expressions

- Can you call me back?
- Give me a call when you're free.
- Call me when you get a chance.
- Could you ring me back?
- Let's talk later.

## Nuance

Common for phone conversations.

## Examples

- Can you call me back?
- Give me a call when you're free.
- Call me when you get a chance.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Use these as fixed conversational phrases.
