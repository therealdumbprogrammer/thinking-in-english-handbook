# Pattern Family 22 — Checking If You Heard Correctly

## Hindi Pattern
- मैंने सही सुना?
- मतलब...?
- तुम कह रहे हो कि...?
- क्या तुमने कहा...?

## Natural English Expressions

- Did I hear that right?
- So, you're saying...?
- Did you say...?
- Just to make sure...

## Nuance

Use these to confirm important information before responding.

## Examples

- Did I hear that right?
- So, you're saying the meeting is tomorrow?
- Just to make sure...

## Common Mistakes

❌ I heard correct?

✅ Did I hear that right?

## Memory Tip

> Confirm before assuming.


## Quick Recap

Most common: See Natural English Expressions above.
