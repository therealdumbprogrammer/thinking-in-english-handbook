# Pattern Family 72 — Asking Who Is Calling

## Hindi Pattern

- कौन बोल रहे हैं?
- कौन हैं?
- कौन बात कर रहे हैं?

## Natural English Expressions

- Who's calling, please?
- May I ask who's calling?
- Who's this?
- Could I ask who's speaking?
- Who's on the line?

## Nuance

Choose formality based on the situation.

## Examples

- Who's calling, please?
- May I ask who's calling?
- Who's this?

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Memorize the whole expression.
