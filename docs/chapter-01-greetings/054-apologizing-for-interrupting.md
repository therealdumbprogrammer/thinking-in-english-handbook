# Pattern Family 54 — Apologizing Before Interrupting

## Hindi Pattern

- बीच में बोलने के लिए माफ़ कीजिए।
- माफ़ करना, बीच में टोक रहा हूँ।

## Natural English Expressions

- Sorry to interrupt.
- Sorry to cut in.
- Excuse me for interrupting.
- May I interrupt for a moment?

## Nuance

Polite interruption.

## Examples

- Sorry to interrupt.
- Sorry to cut in.
- Excuse me for interrupting.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

> Learn the complete expression.
