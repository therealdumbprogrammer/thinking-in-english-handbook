# Pattern Family 39 — Changing the Topic

## Hindi Pattern

- वैसे...
- चलो छोड़ो...
- अच्छा, एक बात...

## Natural English Expressions

- By the way...
- Anyway...
- Speaking of that...
- On another note...
- That reminds me...

## Nuance

Smoothly transition to a new topic.

## Examples

- By the way...
- Anyway...
- Speaking of that...

## Common Mistakes

❌ Leave it.
