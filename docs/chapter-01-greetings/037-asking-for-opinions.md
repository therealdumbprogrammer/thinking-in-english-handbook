# Pattern Family 37 — Asking for Someone's Opinion

## Hindi Pattern

- तुम्हारा क्या कहना है?
- तुम्हें क्या लगता है?
- क्या राय है?

## Natural English Expressions

- What do you think?
- What's your opinion?
- How do you see it?
- What's your take?
- Any thoughts?

## Nuance

Invite the other person to share their view.

## Examples

- What do you think?
- What's your opinion?
- How do you see it?

## Common Mistakes

❌ What's in your mind?

✅ What do you think?

## Memory Tip

> Use fixed opinion phrases.
