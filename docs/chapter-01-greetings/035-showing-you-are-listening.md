# Pattern Family 35 — Showing You're Listening

## Hindi Pattern

- हूँ।
- अच्छा।
- समझ गया।
- हम्म।

## Natural English Expressions

- I see.
- Uh-huh.
- Right.
- Got it.
- I understand.

## Nuance

These listener responses keep the conversation flowing.

## Examples

- I see.
- Uh-huh.
- Right.

## Common Mistakes

❌ Yes only.

✅ I see.

## Memory Tip

> Small acknowledgements sound natural.
