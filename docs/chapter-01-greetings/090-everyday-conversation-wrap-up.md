# Pattern Family 90 — Everyday Conversation Wrap-up

## Hindi Pattern

- ठीक है, फिर बात करेंगे।
- चलो, मिलते हैं।
- ख़याल रखना।

## Natural English Expressions

- Talk to you later.
- Catch you later.
- See you around.
- Take care.
- Until next time.

## Nuance

Natural endings for everyday conversations.

## Examples

- Talk to you later.
- Catch you later.
