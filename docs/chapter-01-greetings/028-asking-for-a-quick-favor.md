# Pattern Family 28 — Asking for a Quick Favor

## Hindi Pattern

- एक काम करोगे?
- एक मदद चाहिए।
- एक छोटा सा काम था।

## Natural English Expressions

- Could you do me a favor?
- Can I ask a quick favor?
- Could you help me with something?
- Would you mind helping me?

## Nuance

Common before making a small request.

## Examples

- Could you do me a favor?
- Can I ask a quick favor?
- Could you help me with something?

## Common Mistakes

❌ Do one work.

✅ Could you do me a favor?

## Memory Tip

> 'Favor' is the natural word.
