# Pattern Family 23 — Asking Someone to Speak More Slowly

## Hindi Pattern
- थोड़ा धीरे बोलिए।
- ज़रा धीरे बोलो।
- बहुत तेज़ बोल रहे हो।

## Natural English Expressions

- Could you speak a little more slowly?
- Would you mind slowing down?
- You're speaking a bit fast.
- Could you slow down a little?

## Nuance

Be polite when asking someone to slow down.

## Examples

- Could you slow down a little?
- You're speaking a bit fast.

## Common Mistakes

❌ Speak slow.

✅ Speak more slowly.

## Memory Tip

> Prefer "slow down" in conversation.

Most common: See Natural English Expressions above.
