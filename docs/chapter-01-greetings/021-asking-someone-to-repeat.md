# Pattern Family 21 — Asking Someone to Repeat

## Hindi Pattern
- फिर से बोलो।
- दोबारा कहना।
- एक बार और बोलोगे?
- क्या कहा?

## Natural English Expressions

- Could you say that again?
- Sorry, could you repeat that?
- I didn't catch that.
- Could you say that one more time?
- Sorry?

## Nuance

Use polite forms in professional settings. "Sorry?" is common in casual conversations.

## Examples

- Sorry, could you repeat that?
- I didn't catch that.
- Could you say that again?

## Common Mistakes

❌ Repeat again.

✅ Could you repeat that?

## Memory Tip

> Ask to repeat **that**, not "again" after repeat.


## Quick Recap

Most common: See Natural English Expressions above.
