# Pattern Family 62 — Asking Someone to Hold

## Hindi Pattern

- लाइन पर रहना।
- कट मत करना।
- रुको, अभी आया।

## Natural English Expressions

- Please hold.
- Hold on a second.
- Stay on the line.
- Don't hang up.
- I'll be right back.

## Nuance

Common during phone calls.

## Examples

- Please hold.
- Hold on a second.
- Stay on the line.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Learn the whole phrase, not individual words.
