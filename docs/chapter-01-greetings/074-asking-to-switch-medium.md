# Pattern Family 74 — Switching Communication Method

## Hindi Pattern

- मैसेज कर दो।
- व्हाट्सऐप पर भेज दो।
- मेल कर देना।

## Natural English Expressions

- Could you text me?
- Can you send it on WhatsApp?
- Could you email me?
- Let's continue over chat.
- Send me a message.

## Nuance

Move the conversation to a better channel.

## Examples

- Could you text me?
- Can you send it on WhatsApp?
- Could you email me?

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Memorize the whole expression.
