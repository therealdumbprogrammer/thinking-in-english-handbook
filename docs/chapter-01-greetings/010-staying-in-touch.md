# Pattern Family 10 — Staying in Touch

## Hindi Pattern

- फिर मिलेंगे।
- संपर्क में रहना।
- मिलते रहना।
- बात करते रहेंगे।

---

## Natural English Expressions

| Expression | Register | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Keep in touch. | Neutral | Most common. |
| See you around. | Casual | Expect to meet again. |
| Talk to you soon. | Neutral | Phone or chat. |
| Let's stay in touch. | Neutral | Friendly. |
| Hope to see you again soon. | Neutral | Warm farewell. |

---

## Nuance

These expressions end a conversation positively without promising a specific future meeting.

---

## Examples

### 💼 Office

**Hindi:** संपर्क में रहना।

**Natural English:** Keep in touch.

### 👥 Friends

**Hindi:** फिर मिलेंगे!

**Natural English:** See you around!

### 📱 Phone

**Hindi:** ठीक है, फिर बात करेंगे।

**Natural English:** Talk to you soon.

### 🎓 Conference

**Hindi:** संपर्क में रहेंगे।

**Natural English:** Let's stay in touch.

### 🏠 Neighbour

**Hindi:** फिर मिलेंगे।

**Natural English:** Hope to see you again soon.

---

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake

We will meet again and again.

### ✅ Natural English

Keep in touch.

### Why?

Native speakers usually express staying connected rather than repeatedly meeting.

---

## Similar English Expressions

| Expression | Difference |
|---|---|
| Catch you later. | Informal. |
| Speak soon. | Phone/chat. |
| Until next time. | Warm farewell. |

---

## Memory Tip

> Think **stay connected**, not **keep meeting**.

**Avoid:** We will meet again and again.
