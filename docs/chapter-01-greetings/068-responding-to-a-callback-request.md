# Pattern Family 68 — Responding to a Callback Request

## Hindi Pattern

- ठीक है, करता हूँ।
- ज़रूर कॉल करूँगा।
- थोड़ी देर में करता हूँ।

## Natural English Expressions

- Sure, I'll call you back.
- I'll give you a call later.
- No problem.
- I'll call once I'm free.
- Sounds good.

## Nuance

Natural responses to callback requests.
