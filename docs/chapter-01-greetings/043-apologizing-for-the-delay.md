# Pattern Family 43 — Apologizing for the Delay

## Hindi Pattern

- देर हो गई।
- लेट हो गया।
- इंतज़ार कराने के लिए माफ़ करना।

## Natural English Expressions

- Sorry I'm late.
- Sorry to keep you waiting.
- Thanks for waiting.
- Apologies for the delay.
- Sorry for the delay.

## Nuance

Keep apologies brief and sincere.

## Examples

- Sorry I'm late.
- Sorry to keep you waiting.
- Thanks for waiting.

## Common Mistakes

See the preferred expressions above instead of translating literally.

## Memory Tip

> Learn the expression as a complete phrase.
