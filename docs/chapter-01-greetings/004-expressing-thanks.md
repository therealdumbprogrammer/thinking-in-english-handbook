# Pattern Family 4 — Expressing Thanks

## Hindi Pattern

- धन्यवाद।
- शुक्रिया।
- बहुत-बहुत धन्यवाद।
- आपका बहुत धन्यवाद।

---

## Natural English Expressions

| Expression | Register | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Thanks. | Casual | Everyday choice. |
| Thank you. | Neutral | Universal. |
| Thanks a lot. | Casual | Stronger appreciation. |
| Thank you so much. | Neutral | Warm gratitude. |
| I really appreciate it. | Neutral | Genuine appreciation. |

---

## Nuance

English speakers say "thanks" much more frequently than Hindi speakers, even for small favors.

---

## Examples

### 🛍️ Shop

**Hindi:** धन्यवाद!

**Natural English:** Thanks!

### 💼 Office

**Hindi:** रिपोर्ट भेजने के लिए धन्यवाद।

**Natural English:** Thank you for sending the report.

### 👥 Friends

**Hindi:** मदद के लिए बहुत-बहुत धन्यवाद।

**Natural English:** Thanks a lot for helping me.

### 🍽️ Restaurant

**Hindi:** खाना बहुत अच्छा था, धन्यवाद।

**Natural English:** Thank you. The food was great.

### 📞 Customer Support

**Hindi:** आपकी मदद के लिए धन्यवाद।

**Natural English:** I really appreciate your help.

---

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake

Many many thanks.

### ✅ Natural English

Thanks a lot.

### Why?

Native speakers don't normally repeat "many".

---

## Similar English Expressions

| Expression | Difference |
|---|---|
| Much appreciated. | Professional. |
| Thanks again. | Repeated gratitude. |
| I owe you one. | Informal; implies returning the favor. |

---

## Memory Tip

> If you're unsure, simply say **Thanks!**

---

## Mini Practice

### Hindi → English

बहुत-बहुत धन्यवाद।

### English → Hindi

I really appreciate it.

---

## Quick Recap

**Most common:** Thanks.

**Most formal:** Thank you.

**Avoid:** Many many thanks.

**Remember:** Thank people often in English.
