# Pattern Family 76 — Asking Someone to Wait on a Call

## Hindi Pattern

- लाइन पर रहिए।
- एक मिनट होल्ड कीजिए।
- कट मत कीजिए।

## Natural English Expressions

- Please hold.
- Could you hold for a moment?
- Stay on the line.
- I'll be right back.
- Please don't hang up.

## Nuance

Use these during phone conversations.

## Examples

- Please hold.
- Could you hold for a moment?
- Stay on the line.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Treat these as complete spoken expressions.
