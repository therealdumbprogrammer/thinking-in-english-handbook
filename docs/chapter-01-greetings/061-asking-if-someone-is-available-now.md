# Pattern Family 61 — Checking Immediate Availability

## Hindi Pattern

- अभी बात कर सकते हैं?
- अभी समय है?
- डिस्टर्ब तो नहीं कर रहा?

## Natural English Expressions

- Is now a good time?
- Do you have a minute?
- Am I catching you at a bad time?
- Can we talk now?
- Are you free to talk?

## Nuance

Check availability before starting a conversation.

## Examples

- Is now a good time?
- Do you have a minute?
- Am I catching you at a bad time?

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Learn the whole phrase, not individual words.
