# Pattern Family 31 — Responding While Thinking

## Hindi Pattern

- हम्म...
- सोचने दो...
- एक मिनट सोचूँ...

## Natural English Expressions

- Let me think.
- Hmm...
- Give me a second to think.
- That's a good question.
- Let me see.

## Nuance

Useful when you need a moment before answering.

## Examples

- Let me think.
- Hmm...
- Give me a second to think.

## Common Mistakes

❌ Wait, I am thinking.

✅ Let me think.

## Memory Tip

> "Let me think" is the standard phrase.
