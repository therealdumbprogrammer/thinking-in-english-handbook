# Pattern Family 63 — Rejoining After a Pause

## Hindi Pattern

- सॉरी, वापस आ गया।
- हाँ, बोलो।
- चलो, आगे बताओ।

## Natural English Expressions

- Sorry about that.
- I'm back.
- Go ahead.
- Where were we?
- Let's continue.

## Nuance

Resume naturally after stepping away.

## Examples

- Sorry about that.
- I'm back.
- Go ahead.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Learn the whole phrase, not individual words.
