# Pattern Family 7 — What Brings You Here?

## Hindi Pattern

- कैसे आना हुआ?
- यहाँ कैसे आना हुआ?
- इधर कैसे?
- आज इधर कैसे?

---

## Natural English Expressions

| Expression | Register | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| What brings you here? | Neutral | Best all-purpose choice. |
| How come you're here? | Casual | Friendly surprise. |
| What are you doing here? | Casual | Tone matters. |
| Fancy seeing you here! | Casual | Expresses pleasant surprise. |
| Didn't expect to see you here! | Casual | Natural reaction. |

---

## Nuance

These expressions express surprise at unexpectedly meeting someone. They usually start a friendly conversation.

---

## Examples

### 🛍️ Mall

**Hindi:** अरे! यहाँ कैसे आना हुआ?

**Natural English:** Hey! What brings you here?

### ☕ Café

**Hindi:** तुम यहाँ?

**Natural English:** Fancy seeing you here!

### ✈️ Airport

**Hindi:** आज इधर कैसे?

**Natural English:** Didn't expect to see you here!

### 💼 Office Lobby

**Hindi:** कैसे आना हुआ?

**Natural English:** What brings you here today?

### 🏥 Hospital

**Hindi:** यहाँ कैसे?

**Natural English:** How come you're here?

---

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake

How did you come here?

### ✅ Natural English

What brings you here?

### Why?

"How did you come?" asks about transportation, not the reason for visiting.

---

## Similar English Expressions

| Expression | Difference |
|---|---|
| Small world! | Coincidental meeting. |
| Nice surprise! | Expresses pleasant surprise. |

---

## Memory Tip

> Think **why**, not **how**.
