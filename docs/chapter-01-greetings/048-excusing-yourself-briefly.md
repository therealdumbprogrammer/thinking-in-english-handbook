# Pattern Family 48 — Excusing Yourself Briefly

## Hindi Pattern

- अभी आया।
- एक मिनट में आता हूँ।
- ज़रा आता हूँ।

## Natural English Expressions

- I'll be right back.
- Excuse me for a moment.
- I'll just be a minute.
- Be right back.
- Give me a moment.

## Nuance

Used when stepping away briefly.

## Examples

- I'll be right back.
- Excuse me for a moment.
- I'll just be a minute.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations; use the standard expressions above.

## Memory Tip

> Learn these as fixed spoken phrases.
