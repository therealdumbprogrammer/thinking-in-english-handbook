# Pattern Family 86 — Inviting Someone into the Conversation

## Hindi Pattern

- आप भी बताइए।
- आपका क्या कहना है?
- आप भी शामिल हों।

## Natural English Expressions

- What do you think?
- Feel free to jump in.
- Would you like to add anything?
- I'd love to hear your thoughts.
- Please join in.

## Nuance

Invite others to participate.

## Examples

- What do you think?
- Feel free to jump in.
- Would you like to add anything?

## Common Mistakes

Avoid literal translations.

## Memory Tip

> Learn the expression as a complete conversational chunk.
