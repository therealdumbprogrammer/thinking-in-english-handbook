# Pattern Family 6 — Meeting After a Long Time

## Hindi Pattern

- बहुत दिनों बाद!
- कितने दिनों बाद मिले!
- कहाँ थे इतने दिन?
- बहुत समय बाद मिले।

---

## Natural English Expressions

| Expression | Register | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Long time no see! | Casual | Most common. |
| It's been a while! | Casual | Warm and natural. |
| It's been ages! | Casual | Exaggerates the gap. |
| Good to see you again! | Neutral | Friendly. |
| Where have you been? | Casual | Usually playful. |

---

## Nuance

These expressions emphasize the happiness of seeing someone again rather than the exact time that has passed.

---

## Examples

### 👥 Friends

**Hindi:** अरे! बहुत दिनों बाद!

**Natural English:** Long time no see!

### 💼 Office

**Hindi:** आपको फिर से देखकर अच्छा लगा।

**Natural English:** Good to see you again.

### 🏫 Reunion

**Hindi:** कितने समय बाद मिले!

**Natural English:** It's been ages!

### 🏠 Neighbour

**Hindi:** कहाँ थे इतने दिन?

**Natural English:** Where have you been?

### 📱 Phone

**Hindi:** बहुत समय बाद बात हो रही है।

**Natural English:** It's been a while!

---

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake

We are meeting after many days.

### ✅ Natural English

Long time no see!

### Why?

English uses idiomatic expressions instead of describing the time gap literally.

---

## Similar English Expressions

| Expression | Difference |
|---|---|
| Nice seeing you again. | Leaving after meeting. |
| Great to see you. | General reunion. |
| Welcome back. | Used after someone's return. |

---

## Memory Tip

> Celebrate the reunion instead of counting the days.

## Quick Recap

**Most common:** Long time no see!

**Most natural:** It's been a while!

**Avoid:** We are meeting after many days.
