# Pattern Family 15 — Checking If Someone Can Hear You

## Hindi Pattern

- मेरी आवाज़ आ रही है?
- सुन पा रहे हो?
- हेलो, सुन रहे हो?
- आवाज़ कट रही है?

## Natural English Expressions

- Can you hear me?
- Can you hear me clearly?
- Are you able to hear me?
- Is my voice breaking up?
- Can you hear me okay?

## Nuance

Used mainly during phone or video calls to confirm audio quality.

## Examples

- Can you hear me?
- Can you hear me clearly?
- Is my voice breaking up?

## Common Mistakes

❌ Is my voice coming?

✅ Can you hear me?

## Memory Tip

> Ask what the listener can hear, not what your voice is doing.
